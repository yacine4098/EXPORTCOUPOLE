========================================
  REPLACE THESE IMAGES WITH YOUR OWN
========================================

Place your custom images in this 'public' folder:

1. LOGO
   File name: logo.png
   Recommended size: 200x80px (or similar aspect ratio)
   Format: PNG (with transparent background) or JPG
   
   This logo appears in:
   - Navigation bar (top of all pages)
   - Footer (bottom of all pages)

2. HERO IMAGE
   File name: hero-image.jpg
   Recommended size: 1920x1080px or larger (for high quality)
   Format: JPG or PNG
   
   This image appears as:
   - Homepage hero section background
   - Make sure it's high quality for best appearance

========================================
HOW TO UPDATE:
========================================

1. Prepare your logo image:
   - Save as: logo.png (or logo.jpg)
   - Place in: d:\algiers-export-hub-main\public\

2. Prepare your hero background image:
   - Save as: hero-image.jpg (or hero-image.png)
   - Place in: d:\algiers-export-hub-main\public\

3. Refresh your browser - changes appear immediately!

Note: Images in the 'public' folder are served directly,
so you can replace them anytime without rebuilding the project.
